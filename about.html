CLEARWATER POOLS

Clearwater Pools a sole next generation ISO 9001-2008 certified Swimming Pool & Landscape organization with nearly 3 decade experience in Design, Construction and Consulting services.

The company was started in 1993 by Late Mr. M.C. Simon as a Proprietorship company. It later became a Private Ltd Company in the year 2011. Our areas of expertise also covers supply and service of a complete range of Swimming Pool equipment for both domestic and commercial purpose. It operates with vibrant, dynamic, seasoned and extremely accountable and service-minded attitudinal resources on the urge to always ensure our clients feel extremely special.

We would like to state emphatically that Architects have always been a boon companions in offering seamless projects with a finest association. Both the client’s ideas and the Architect’s planning are clubbed together to bring up the best result. Nevertheless retaining our existing Architects, their clients or our direct ones, Clearwater pools has always been bestowed with keeping our new Clients and Architects as comfortable and compatible as the former through our extremely reliable services. Our projects are both economical and super innovative with accurate, descriptive technicalities and trend setting pool equipment.

Clearwater pools has never failed to offer hassle free execution and post project service with a promising Project Management model right from requirement Analysis, Planning, Execution, Monitor- Control and Closure of the same effective. It does not aim to just acquire new projects and yet ultimately concentrates on it’s brandship and so consciously ensures the affinity to all our Clients and business owners for a stupendous work satisfaction. We believe in providing value for money.

Our past successes and current demands have enabled us to expand operations outside of our base in Chennai to the promising cities in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh, Coimbatore, Pondicherry, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Goa, Andaman & Nicobar Islands and more. We are open to taking Projects across Pan India and extend our services too.
